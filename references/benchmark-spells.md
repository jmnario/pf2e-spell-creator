# Benchmark Spells per Rank — PF2e Remaster

Anchor spells for every rank. When designing a custom spell, find the closest match here and calibrate against it. All formulas are from Archives of Nethys (Remaster where applicable).

Format: **Name** — traits — range/area — defense — damage/effect — heighten — role

---

## Cantrips (Rank "Cantrip 1")

- **Electric Arc** — concentrate, electricity, manipulate — 30 ft, 2 creatures — basic Reflex — **2d4 electricity** — +1d4/+1 — *gold-standard multi-target cantrip; the benchmark for "balanced cantrip damage."*
- **Telekinetic Projectile** — attack, concentrate, manipulate — 30 ft — spell attack — **2d6 B/P/S** (object-dependent) — +1d6/+1 — *single-target spell-attack cantrip benchmark.*
- **Ignition** — attack, concentrate, fire, manipulate — 30 ft ranged or melee reach — spell attack — **2d4 ranged / 2d6 melee fire**; crit +1d4 persistent — +1 die/+1 — *risk/reward cantrip; melee variant rivals martials.*
- **Daze** — concentrate, manipulate, mental — 60 ft — basic Will — **1d6 mental** + stunned 1 on crit fail — +1d6/+2 — *control-focused cantrip; Will save, slower heighten.*
- **Live Wire** — attack, concentrate, electricity, manipulate, metal — 30 ft — spell attack — **1d4 slashing + 1d4 electricity**; crit +1d4 persistent electricity — +1d4 each/+2 — *dual-damage cantrip; persistent rider justifies +2 cadence.*
- **Vitality Lash** — concentrate, manipulate, vitality — 30 ft — basic Fortitude — **2d6 vitality** + enfeebled 1 on crit fail — +1d6/+1 — *anti-undead benchmark.*
- **Void Warp** — concentrate, manipulate, void — 30 ft — basic Fortitude — **2d4 void** + enfeebled 1 on crit fail — +1d4/+1 — *anti-living counterpart; smaller dice because no restriction.*
- **Breathe Fire** — concentrate, fire, manipulate — 15-ft cone — basic Reflex — **2d6 fire** — +2d6/+1 — *AoE cantrip; double dice on heighten for cone shape.*
- **Shield** — abjuration cantrip — self, 1 round, AC +1 + Hardness 5 (raises by rank) — *defensive cantrip; 10-min cooldown if Shield Block is used.*

## Rank 1

- **Force Barrage (Magic Missile)** — concentrate, force, manipulate — 120 ft — **no save, auto-hit** — **1d4+1 force per shard** (1/2/3 shards by 1/2/3 actions) — +1 shard per +2 ranks — *gold-standard unconditional damage; force is rarely resisted.*
- **Hydraulic Push** — concentrate, manipulate, water — 60 ft — spell attack — **3d6 bludgeoning + 5-ft knockback** (6d6 + 10 ft on crit) — +2d6/+1 — *single-target attack-roll benchmark with rider.*
- **Fear** — concentrate, emotion, fear, manipulate, mental — 30 ft, 1 creature — Will (non-basic) — no damage; **frightened 1/2/3 + fleeing** by save degree — Heightened (3rd) up to 5 creatures — *iconic save-or-suck without damage.*
- **Mystic Armor (Mage Armor)** — concentrate, manipulate — self, until next daily prep — +1 item bonus to AC — Heightened (4th) also +1 item to saves; (6th) AC becomes +2, saves +1; (8th) AC +2, saves +2; (10th) AC +3, saves +3 — *defensive utility benchmark.*
- **Heal** — concentrate, healing, manipulate, vitality — touch / 30 ft / 30-ft emanation — basic Fort (vs undead only) — **1d8 HP** (+8 if 2-action variant) or 1d8 vitality damage to undead — +1d8/+1, +8 to 2-action — *healing benchmark.*
- **Sleep** — concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental, sleep — 30 ft, 5-ft burst — Will (non-basic) — fall asleep — *iconic incapacitation example.*
- **Grease** — concentrate, manipulate — 60 ft, 4 squares or 1 object — Reflex/Acrobatics — fall prone on fail, drop item if held — *low-rank battlefield control.*

## Rank 2

- **Acid Grip (Acid Arrow)** — concentrate, acid, manipulate — 120 ft, 1 creature — Reflex (non-basic; success = half, no persistent, pulled 5 ft; fail = full + persistent, pulled 10 ft; crit fail = double + persistent, pulled 20 ft) — **2d8 acid + 1d6 persistent acid** with a -10-ft speed penalty while persistent damage continues — +2d8 + 1d6 persistent per +2 — *persistent damage benchmark.*
- **Mirror Image** — illusion, visual — self, 1 min — 3 images; ~20% chance per image attack misses — *defensive illusion benchmark.*
- **Invisibility** — illusion — touch, willing — undetected until hostile action; 10 min — Heightened (4th) lasts 1 min, doesn't end on hostile action — *iconic stealth benchmark.*
- **See the Unseen (See Invisibility)** — divination — self, 10 min — see invisible creatures as concealed; sense undetected as hidden — *utility benchmark.*

## Rank 3

- **Fireball** — concentrate, fire, manipulate — 500 ft, 20-ft burst — basic Reflex — **6d6 fire** — +2d6/+1 — *the canonical AoE damage benchmark; every custom AoE compares to this.*
- **Lightning Bolt** — concentrate, electricity, manipulate — 120-ft line — basic Reflex — **4d12 electricity** — +1d12/+1 — *line variant; bigger dice, fewer dice, tighter shape.*
- **Haste** — concentrate, manipulate — 30 ft, 1 willing — 1 min — +1 quickened action (Strike/Stride only) — Heightened (7th) up to 6 targets — *iconic buff benchmark.*
- **Slow** — concentrate, manipulate — 30 ft, 1 creature — Fortitude (non-basic) — **slowed 1 (1 round) / slowed 1 (1 min) / slowed 2 (1 min)** — Heightened (6th) up to 10 creatures — *single-target debuff benchmark.*
- **Paralyze** — concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental — 120 ft — Will — paralyzed by degree — *iconic incapacitation control.*
- **Heroism** — divination, blessing — touch, 10 min — +1 status to attacks, Perception, saves, skills — Heightened (6th) +2; Heightened (9th) +3 — *status-bonus benchmark.*
- **Stinking Cloud** — concentrate, manipulate, poison — 120 ft, 20-ft burst — Fortitude (non-basic) — sickened 1–2 + cloud persists — Heightened (5th) +duration — *area control benchmark.*
- **Vampiric Feast (Vampiric Touch)** — concentrate, death, manipulate, void — touch, 1 living creature — basic Fortitude — **6d6 void**; caster gains temp HP equal to half the void damage dealt — +2d6/+1 — *touch-range life drain; the canonical Vampiric Touch rename.*

## Rank 4

- **Wall of Fire** — concentrate, fire, manipulate — 120 ft, 60-ft wall (or 10-ft-radius ring), 5 ft thick — no save (crossing/starting in deals damage) — **4d6 fire** — +1d6/+1 — *sustained zone damage; slower heighten.*
- **Confusion** — concentrate, emotion, incapacitation, manipulate, mental — 30 ft — Will (non-basic) — no damage; **stunned 1 / confused 1 min (save-end) / confused 1 min (no save)** — Heightened (8th) up to 10 creatures — *iconic save-or-suck.*
- **Weapon Storm** — concentrate, manipulate — 30-ft cone or 10-ft emanation — basic Reflex — **4 dice of weapon damage die, weapon type** — +1 die/+1 — *unique because dice size varies by weapon.*
- **Fly** — concentrate, manipulate — touch, 5 min — fly Speed = land Speed — *utility benchmark.*
- **Vampiric Maiden** — concentrate, manipulate, void — 30 ft, 1 creature — Fortitude — **4d4 piercing + 4d4 void**; caster gains temp HP equal to void damage taken (1 min) — +1d4 each/+1 — *rank-4 life-drain benchmark; distinct from Vampiric Feast.*

---

## Rank 5

- **Howling Blizzard (Cone of Cold)** — air, cold, concentrate, manipulate — 60-ft cone (2-action) OR 500 ft, 30-ft burst (3-action) — basic Reflex — **10d6 cold** + difficult terrain — +2d6/+1 — *rank-5 AoE benchmark.*
- **Banishment** — concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate — 30 ft, 1 creature off home plane — Will — no damage; banished by degree — Heightened (9th) up to 10 creatures — *iconic save-or-banish.*
- **Death Ward** — abjuration — touch, 10 min — resistance 10 to void; protection vs death effects — *defensive benchmark vs Massacre and necromancy.*
- **Wall of Stone** — concentrate, earth, manipulate — 120 ft, 120 ft long, 20 ft high — sustained or permanent — per 10×10 section: AC 10, Hardness 14, 50 HP — *permanent-barrier benchmark.*
- **Slither (Black Tentacles)** — concentrate, manipulate, shadow — 120 ft, 20-ft burst — Reflex (non-basic; success = unaffected, fail = full damage + grabbed, crit fail = double damage + restrained) — **3d6 piercing + 1d6 persistent poison** from shadow-snake bites — Heightened (+2) persistent poison +1d6 and snake HP +6 — *grab/restraint benchmark; now shadow-flavored.*
- **Teleport** — concentrate, manipulate, teleportation — up to 4 willing, 100 mi — *teleport benchmark.*

---

## Rank 6

- **Disintegrate** — attack, concentrate, manipulate — 120 ft, 1 creature/object — spell attack + basic Fortitude (crit hit = save one degree worse) — **12d10** untyped — +2d10/+1 — *single-target attack-roll damage king; "rocket tag" benchmark.*
- **Chain Lightning** — concentrate, electricity, manipulate — 500 ft, chains 30 ft — basic Reflex — **8d12 electricity** (single shared roll); chain breaks on any crit success — +1d12/+1 — *multi-target chain benchmark.*
- **True Seeing** — divination — self, 10 min — see through illusions/disguises — *utility benchmark.*
- **Scrying** — divination, mental, scrying — long range, 1 minute — observe a creature you can name — *iconic divination.*
- **Dominate** — concentrate, incapacitation, manipulate, mental — 30 ft — Will — controlled by degree — *iconic incapacitation.*

---

## Rank 7

- **Fiery Body** — concentrate, fire, manipulate, polymorph — self, 1 min — immune to fire, weakness 5 cold/water; touch attackers take 3d6 fire; unarmed +1d4 fire; fire spells +1 die — Heightened (9th) bigger dice + fly 60 — *self-buff polymorph benchmark.*
- **Plane Shift** — concentrate, manipulate, teleportation — touch, 8 willing — planar travel — *utility benchmark.*
- **Mask of Terror** — concentrate, emotion, fear, illusion, manipulate, mental — 30 ft, 1 willing, 1 min — Will save vs anyone attacking target; frightened by degree — Heightened (8th) up to 5 creatures — *fear-buff benchmark.*
- **Sunburst** — concentrate, fire, light, manipulate, vitality — 500 ft, 60-ft burst — Reflex (non-basic: no effect / half / full / full + permanent blindness on crit fail) — **8d10 fire + 8d10 vitality vs undead** — +1d10 each/+1 — *big-radius AoE with anti-undead rider.*

---

## Rank 8

- **Desiccate (Horrid Wilting)** — concentrate, manipulate, void — 500 ft, any number of living creatures — basic Fortitude — **10d10 void** to living; plants/water creatures take one degree worse — +1d10/+1 — *target-list AoE benchmark; uncapped target count, range-limited only.*
- **Mind Blank** — abjuration, mental — touch, 24 hr — immune to detection, scrying, mental info-gathering — *iconic high-end mental defense.*
- **Quandary (Maze)** — concentrate, extradimensional, incapacitation, manipulate, teleportation — 30 ft, 1 creature — Will — sends target to extraplanar puzzle; escape via skill check vs spell DC — *iconic single-target removal.*
- **Polar Ray** — attack, cold, concentrate, manipulate — 120 ft — spell attack — **10d8 cold + drained 2** on hit (double on crit) — +2d8/+1 — *rank-8 attack-roll benchmark; drained 2 is a major rider.*

---

## Rank 9

- **Falling Stars (Meteor Swarm)** — concentrate, manipulate — 500 ft, four 40-ft bursts with 10-ft inner bursts — basic Reflex — **6d10 bludgeoning** (inner) + **14d6 chosen energy** (sonic/fire/cold/electricity, outer) — +1d10 + 2d6/+1 — *ultimate AoE benchmark.*
- **Foresight** — divination, blessing — touch, 1 hour — +2 status to initiative; not off-guard to undetected; rolls saves twice (fortune) — *defensive divination benchmark.*
- **Massacre** — concentrate, death, manipulate, void — 60-ft line; living creatures ≤level 17 — Fortitude (non-basic) — **9d6 void on success / 100 on fail / death on crit fail**; 30 void backfire if no kill — Heightened (10th) ≤level 19, 10d6 / 115 / death — *iconic save-or-die benchmark.*

---

## Rank 10

- **Cataclysm** — acid, air, cold, concentrate, earth, electricity, fire, manipulate, water — 1,000 ft, 60-ft burst — basic Reflex — **7 × 3d10 mixed types (~115)**; treats resistances as 10 lower — no heighten — *peak AoE; multiple types defeat resistance.*
- **Wish** (Remaster: rank-10 **ritual**, rare) — 100,000 gp components, 1 day to cast — reality alteration — *utility cap.*
- **Remake** — restoration ritual, rare — bring back creature long dead from a small remnant — *resurrection cap.*
